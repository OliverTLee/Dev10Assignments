About Me

I am a recent graduate of the University of Minnesota , with a bachelor's degree in Bioproducts and Biosystems Engineering, with a minor in chemistry.

Coding and software development has become an interest of mine as I have come to realize that computers are the new frontier of human development. New systems are implemented into our lives every year, that automate our lives. New products are controlling our houses heating, updating our grocery lists, suggesting friends we haven't seen since middle school, and (hopefully) soon driving our cars. The sky is the limit, and I want to be a part of it.
