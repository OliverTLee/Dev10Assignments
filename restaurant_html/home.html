Restaurant Description
The Krusty Krab is a renowned local dining destination, and home of the one and only Krabby Patty. All food is cooked to order and never frozen! A much better alternative to the chumbucket, an awful restaurant that doesn't even have it's own original recipes. The Krusty Krab was founded in 1905 when founder Eugene Krabs bought the retirement home the Rusty Krab, and rebranded it as a high quality fast food restaurant. The Krusty Krab is clean and disease free. The Krusty Krab supports local artists, and has featured local artists, that have been studying the clarinet and mopping for years.
